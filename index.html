About Us

Hotel Haritaj Varanasi — Experience Comfort & Elegance

Welcome to Hotel Haritaj, Varanasi — where comfort, culture, and warm hospitality come together in the timeless city of Kashi. Located near the Heritage Institute of Medical Sciences and the city’s key routes, we are a trusted choice for travelers seeking peace, convenience, and exceptional service.

Inspired by the rich spiritual heritage of Varanasi and complemented by modern elegance, we offer rooms, dining, and event spaces designed to make every stay memorable. Whether you are visiting for a spiritual journey, business, leisure, or a family celebration, our dedicated team is committed to making every moment special.
